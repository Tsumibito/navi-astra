Our mission: To make yachting accessible by helping everyone realise their dream of sailing.

Navi.training is your reliable partner in the challenging world of yachting. We will help you realise your dream of the sea, teach you how to sail almost any yacht, organise an unforgettable sailing holiday, deliver your yacht anywhere in the world and help you make the right choice when buying a vessel.

We create state-of-the-art yachting training materials to simplify access to yachtsman training and give you confidence on the water. 

We make unforgettable sailing holidays affordable by helping our clients to charter a yacht in any region of the world, with or without a skipper.

We develop the yachting community and make yachting closer and clearer for a wide range of people of all ages and professions.
