What's the bottom line?

Why do boaters choose ISSA? 

It is a recognised certification system for recreational skippers. 

ISSA was created in 1969 through the efforts of Les Glenans (FRA), Polish Yachting Association (POL), Yacht Club de Morges (SUI), Casa di Vela Caprera (ITA), National Schools Sailing Association (GBR) and Centro Internacional de Navigacion de Arousa (ESP). The organisation sets and monitors the standards of yachting education.

Its certificates are accepted in most countries of the world. 
Join the yachting community and discover new horizons with ISSA!

Inshore Skipper Sail

Skipper's Certificate to operate a sailing yacht with motor up to 24 metres, with Max GT 20, up to 20 nm from shore, day and night.

This is a PERMANENT certificate and does not need to be renewed every 5 years.

  • Minimum age requirement: 16 years
  • Certification required for training: No
  • Nautical experience required: Preferably 1 week of cruising under sail

You can complete this programme at Navi.training sailing school

Offshore Skipper Sail

This skipper's certificate extends your ability to sail a motor yacht up to 24 metres, from Max GT to 50, on the high seas and up to 60 miles from shore, with no restrictions on weather or time of day.

This is a PERMANENT certificate and does not need to be renewed every 5 years.

  • Minimum age: 18 years.
  • Skipper experience: 28 days at sea as captain or senior duty officer, with a minimum of 2 voyages and at least 2 sea areas.
  • Mandatory pre-training certificates: SRC VHF and first aid certificate.

You can complete this programme at Navi.training sailing school

Book the course

Master of Yacht

This prestigious and respected skipper's certificate confirms your extensive experience and enables you to sail a sailboat up to 24 metres, with Max GT up to 200, on the open sea and ocean with no limits on distance from shore, no restrictions on weather or time of day.

This is a PERMANENT certificate and does not need to be renewed every 5 years.

  • Minimum age required: 18 years old
  • Certification required prior training: VHF SRC, First Aid Certificate, Sea Survival
  • Experience required prior training: 60 days at sea as skipper or watch leader in at least 5 voyages, at least in 3 different sea areas, including tidal waters (at least one area), 2 passages over 100 nautical miles as skipper

You can complete this programme at Navi.training sailing school

Yacht Crew

This is a certificate for a qualified crew member who is familiar with the basics of yachting and is able to assist the skipper on moorings on yachts up to 24 metres. Some charters companies require, in addition to the skipper of the yacht, the presence of such a qualified sailor in the crew.

This is a LIFETIME certificate and does not need to be renewed every 5 years.

  • Minimum age required: 10 years old
  • Certification required prior training: None
  • Experience required prior training: None

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Our head office is located in La Rochelle, France. Our address can be found in the footer of the website. We also have training bases in Turkey, Montenegro and Spain, from where our offshore practice groups usually leave.

Most of our core courses consist of theory, which is completed online, and offshore practice. The theory contains the required training material for your chosen course, usually a series of recorded lectures lasting 15-30 min each, supported by tests or practical assignments if necessary. Also in between modules we organise ZOOM meetings between cadets and instructors for more in-depth control of mastering the material and answers to arising questions. Sea practice is held in small groups, depending on the chosen training and format it will be from 2 to 4 cadets on the yacht + skipper-instructor. As a rule, the sea practice lasts one charter week (from Saturday afternoon to the following Friday evening) and takes place in intensive training mode. After successfully passing the practical examination we start the procedure of issuing the skipper's certificate, which usually takes 1-2 months.

MON NAVI SARL is a French company based in Lagord in the Charente-Maritime dapartment on the Atlantic coast of France. We usually accept SEPA payments in Euros to our IBAN account or card payments via Stripe or Pay Pal.

Our instructors are certified by the European Association of Yacht Schools ISSA and we issue Competent Crew, Inshore Skipper Sail, Offshore Skipper Sail, Master of Yacht, Inshore Power Skipper, Offshore Power Skipper and VHF SRC operator certificates.
